HINGES 2019 THEME

There is no such thing as ‘AWAY’, when we throw everything away, it must go somewhere. HINGES 19’ is back again with a gigantic and an alarming theme, ‘CARBON EMISSIONS’ . Do you all know that the world emits more than 2.4 million pounds of CO2 per second? Carbon emissions and there is no planet B.So we are coming up with a theme which focuses on the prevention of waste that encourages the redesign of RESOURCE life cycles so that all products are REUSED and with the emphasis on ‘CARBON FOOTPRINT’. It strives to ensure that products are designed to be REPAIRED, REFURBISHED, RE-MANUFACTURED and generally REUSED.

Carbon emissions contribute to climate change, which can have serious consequences for humans and their environment. The role of a civil engineer is to work towards solutions for safe, effective management through the implementation of specialist recycling processes and strategies by designing structures that emit fewer of the greenhouse gases during construction, throughout building use and at the end of the building life.So, get ready to experience the uniqueness. ‘LETS START BEING MORE EFFICIENT FROM TODAY. Looking forward to see you all as HINGES’19 is ready to give you an electrifying experience.
