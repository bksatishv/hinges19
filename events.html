Aestimatio valere

This event is open for all those who have an unexplained passion for estimating structures and valuation. Ever wondered View Details tender estimate , here is your chance to bind it. An opportunity to test your estimating skill.

Aestimatio valere

Rules and regulations

  • Team must consist of 1-3 participants
  • The decision of the judges shall be final

 

Round 1 :

  • preliminary round which consist of a written MCQ test based on your basic estimation and valuation knowledge
  • Time limit : 30 mins

 

Round 2 :

  • Real world structures are given for estimating and valuation.
  • Time limit : 90 mins

 

Judgement criteria

  • 5-6 teams will be selected  based on how closer their estimated value is to the real value.

 

 

Round 3 :

  • Final round in which the shortlisted teams are supposed to bind a tender for a given site based on description which will be given on the spot.
  • The judges decision are final and binding 
  • Time limit : 90 mins.

 

Judgement criteria

Least valid tender will win

Paper Presentation

It is now time for you to bring your innovations to light! Muster what is out of the ordinary and tell us what you have got to bring a revolution in the Industry. Present your ideas and win your prize!

Paper Presentation

Presentation topic

Any topic related to Civil Engineering.

Format

An abstract must be sent to events.hinges@sastra.ac.in before 3rd September 2017 9:00 PM. An abstract should have not more than 500 words. The shortlisted participants have to present their paper along with a PPT in front of the judges.

Rules & Regulations

• Number of participants per team: 2-3
• Maximum duration: 10 minutes ( 8 minutes for presentation and 2 minutes for queries)
• Judges decision is final and binding.

Note:

Participants are requested to send the soft copy of their paper in IEEE format to events.hinges@sastra.ac.in
If paper gets selected, the shortlisted teams are requested to submit four hard copies of their paper in IEEE format on the day of presentation and also mail the final presentation file to events.hinges@sastra.ac.in before 7th September 2017 6 PM
